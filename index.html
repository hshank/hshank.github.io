What I've been up to lately...



Project RISHI (Rural India Social and Health Improvement) is a non-profit organization whose mission is to promote the sustainable development and growth of rural Indian communities. I currently serve on the National Team as Director of Special Projects. My involvemnet with the organization reaches back to 2014.

I have served as the Education Project Lead and have led initiatives to better school infrastructure in the village, provide innovative ways for kids to read and write, and connect students to higher education institutions.




This past summer I represented Project RISHI and went to a village Bharog Bhaneri in India. After my trip, a couple of friends and I travelled around India and Singapore. Check out the video my friend made!

During the summer of 2015, I interned as a Software Engineer at Microsoft in Redmond, Washington. I created a new feature for the Start Screen for Windows 10, on phone, tablet, and desktop.


In March 2016, I went to Cabo, Mexico for Spring Break with a bunch of my friends! I highly recommend this trip for any college seniors, looking to do something fun for their last spring break. Check out the video!
